Project GREET

The three components of Project GREET are engaging communities, empowering girls and training audiences on girl rights; the project examines human trafficking, child marriage and gender equality in the US and worldwide.

The Issue Addressed

This project aims to address the following—trafficking, child marriage and the lack of women's rights.

Watch the Films

These films educate audiences on the above topic, with a wide array of sources and through interviews with experts in the field.
