United Nations

Sakshi Satpathy was a Student Delegate at the United Nations-Commission on the Status of Women 62nd session (UN-CSW 62) from March 12-16, 2018 (http://www.unwomen.org/en/csw/csw62-2018)
She met and discussed with the following global leaders :
  1. Ms Phumzile Mlambo-Ngcuka, Executive Director, UN Women
  2. Ms Geraldine Byrne Nason, Chair of UN Commission on Status of Women 62 (UN-CSW62), Ambassador, Permanent Representative of Ireland to the United Nations
  3. Ms Jaha Dukureh, UN Women's Regional Goodwill Ambassador for Africa and Nobel Peace Prize Nominee
  4. Dr. Sania Nishtar, Commission Chair of WHO Independent High-level Commission on NCDs, World Health Organization
  5. Dr. Hessa Al-Jaber, former Minister of Communication and Information Technology of the State of Qatar, United Nations ITU Broadband Commissioner for Digital Development, Commissioner for the WHO/ITU Commission on Information and Accountability for Women and Children's Health
  6. Ms Angelica Spraggins, Communications Officer, World Health Organization (WHO)
  7. Ms Fleur Newman, United Nations Climate Change Secretariat (UNFCCC), Gender Focal Point, Office of the Deputy Executive Secretary, Executive Direction and Management, Germany
  8. Mr. Nontawat Chandrtri, Ambassador and Deputy Permanent Representative of Thailand to the United Nations

  10. Ms. Doma Tshering, Permanent Representative of Bhutan to the United Nations
